Visualization

Reading out report of several pages about hashtags would take time. Graphs provide more fast information, saving time. Hashtags have been visualized in two kinds of charts. Firs one is bar chart and second one is pie chart. Bar chart shows information in bars and pie chart shows percentage of items. Mentions have been also visualized in same two charts. For likes and retweets line chart has been used. Word frequency has been visualized in two charts named as wordcloud and frequency distribution chart. Wordcloud can be used by import wordcloud library and later can be used by NLTK functions. Further Bigram networks are provided for mapping network of cooccurrence of words.
